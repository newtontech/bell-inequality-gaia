A theorem

Einstein,

They concluded

He showed that in a, Gedankenexperiment

that of EPR) no local hidden-variable

of quantum
mechanics.
This result is somewhat

that quantum

systems are incompatible
with his conditions

ity unless
hidden variables

Bell's theorem

tire family of local hidden-variable

The aim of this paper is to propose

such an
